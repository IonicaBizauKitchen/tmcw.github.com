Entropy is transcendent: it is both the ruling state of reality and exceptionally difficult to explain and to capture intentionally. Let's look close and far and see where we get.

## Entropy in Computers

Electrically, we design computer hardware to be thoroughly deterministic - from one state of transistors on and off and a certain instruction in the CPU, we should always arrive at the same next step.
